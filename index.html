About Thippi

Food, People & the Heart of Thailand

Thippawan Rashleigh, Thippi grew up in a small village northeastern of Thailand, Chaiyaphum with food as her love language. Now based in St Albans, she brings the soul of her hometown to every dish — using Thai herbs and spices, seasonal produce, and locally sourced ingredients to create flavours that are both deeply authentic and beautifully of-the-moment. Thippi is passionate about sustainability: cook what you have at home, in the right portions, and create no waste.

Her three passions — people, food, and yoga — shape everything she does. Whether she's cooking for first-time visitors or old friends, running a class, or writing her debut cookbook, Thippi's mission is always the same: connection through food.

Forthcoming Book

A Cookbook
That Crosses Borders

Thippi is currently writing her first book — a deeply personal collection of Thai recipes adapted for life outside Thailand. Expect vibrant dishes, honest storytelling, and guidance on how to find (or substitute) authentic Thai ingredients wherever you live.

From street food she grew up eating to her family recipe which carries a memory. This is not a restaurant cookbook — it's an invitation into a Thai home kitchen, no matter where in the world you happen to be.
